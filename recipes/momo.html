Mo:Mo Recipe

Mo:Mo

I am from Nepal and when you ask people what you like the most many people's answer are same mo:mo.
There are many varieties of mo:mo but I like buff mo:mo it always buff mo:mo for me.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Buff meat
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Coriander
  • Spices

Steps for making mo:mo

  1. Cut onions, garlic, ginger, coriander into small pieces.
  2. Grind garlic and ginder to make a paste.
  3. Grind the buff meat.
  4. Now mix everything that you prepared.
  5. Now prepare the dough. make thin, small circle sizes.
  6. Now stuff that mixing in each circle dough and wrap it.
  7. Steam it and eat it.
